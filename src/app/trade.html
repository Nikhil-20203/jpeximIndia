CORPORATE PROTOCOLS

Trade Terms, Compliance & Banking Protocols

JP EXIM operates under globally recognized commercial standards. We prioritize financial transparency, strict contract adherence, and robust regulatory compliance with Indian agricultural export bodies to ensure worry-free corporate imports.

1. Delivery Terms (Incoterms 2020)

We support multiple International Chamber of Commerce (ICC) delivery profiles to match the logistical demands of your corporate team:

  • •
    FOB (Free On Board): Mundra Port or Kandla Port. Highly cost-effective for buyers with existing freight contracts.
  • •
    CIF (Cost, Insurance & Freight): We arrange secure booking to Jebel Ali (UAE), Dammam (Saudi Arabia), or other global destination ports.
  • •
    CFR (Cost and Freight): Safe ocean transit to custom ports with buyer managing marine insurance coverage.
account_balance

2. Payment Methods & Banking

We prioritize mutual protection, working only with world-renowned banking institutions through standard verified instruments:

  • •
    Irrevocable Letter of Credit (L/C): Confirmed and payable at sight, issued by a tier-1 international bank.
  • •
    Telegraphic Transfer (T/T): Standard advanced deposit (e.g., 30% advance deposit upon contract signing, and 70% balance against scanned copies of the original Bill of Lading).
  • •
    Custom Trade Profiles: Terms for established long-term regular importers can be negotiated individually.

3. Export Certifications & Assurances

Our agricultural dispatches are systematically backed by essential regulatory approvals:

  • •
    Phytosanitary Certificate: Issued by the Ministry of Agriculture, India, guaranteeing pest-free fresh crops.
  • •
    SGS / Surveyor Quality Certification: Third-party verification of weight, size, and aflatoxin presence at port of loading.
  • •
    Certificate of Origin: Authenticated proof of rich crop sourcing from premium Gujarat fields.
